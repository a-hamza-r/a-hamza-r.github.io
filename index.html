About me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at Computer Science Department, Florida State University and a member of the Formal Methods Lab. I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Grigory Fedyukovich.

My area of research interests and expertise are: Formal Methods, Program Analysis, Software Verification, and Compilers. My current focus is on Equivalence Checking of Programs. I am also working on Verification of eBPF programs (Linux Kernel extensions).
